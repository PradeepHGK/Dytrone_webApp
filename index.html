WE ARE Scot International

BEST FINANCIAL ADVISOR FOR YOUR FUTURE BUSINESS SUCCESS TO GROW UP

Our recommendations are used in financial instruments that are used by traders, companies and individuals to meet required trade finance needs. Scot International has global presence and knowledge that shall help to provide a solution that is flexible and cognizant of the needs of our clients.

We are specialists in Project Funding across the world and will give you the enjoyment of our Rapid Service, Copetitive fees and a high client satisfaction rates.

MISSION

We collect and interpret financial information to providing quality customer services. We strive to protect the financial integrity of the Customer and Investor to help in business growth in a regulatory, technological and growth oriented environment.

VISION

Our philosophy is to shape up and contribute towards the growth of efficient business and not just funding.



OUR SERVICES

PROVIDE BEST SERVICES

SBLC

Our rapid credit analysis process, customer engagement and ability to advice quality collaterals which enable us to get you the compliance and brief underwriting duties to ensure the credit quality of the party seeking the letter of credit, then send notification to the bank or investor party requesting the LC (a seller or creditor).

LC

We have the ability to obtain LC that shall be payable immediately – within five to ten days – after the seller meets the requirements of the letter of credit.




PROJECT FUNDING

With our long-term loans, we offer you tailor-made funding solutions at matching maturities for your company worldwide. Acting as arranger of syndicated financings, we can also mobilise other financing partners and offer a joint financing package.

VENTURE CAPITALISTS

We identify the unique requirements of any venture capital fund requirements. We understand your specific needs and delivered through our client-centric model supported by state-of-the-art technology platforms with a multi-jurisdictional operational capability.
